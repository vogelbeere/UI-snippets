Pull quote

A pullquote is typically used in articles to draw attention to a particular phrase, topic, or fact. Pullquotes (also sometimes referred to as lift-out or call-out quotes) are usually set in a font that is larger and more visible than paragraph text; body copy is typically wrapped around the quote.
